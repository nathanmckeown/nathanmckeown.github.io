THE SINKING OF THE TITANIC



MAIDEN VOYAGE

The Titanic sank in the North Atlantic ocean on its way to New York on its maiden voyage.







ICEBERG

After hitting the iceberg it took The Titanic 2 hours and 40 minutes to sink.







LIFEBOATS

53% of the people on the Titanic could have survived using the lifeboats. Only 31% did as lots of the lifeboats had empty seats.







ORCHESTRA

While sinking, the orchestra kept playing music to calm down the people on board.
