THERE ARE LOTS OF ANIMALS THAT LIVE IN THE RAINFOREST

Some of the most amazing creatures live in Rainforests and some of the most strangest do as well.



PINK RIVER DOLPHIN

The Amazon River is the only place to find the Amazon Pink River Dolphin, the rarest dolphin in the world.







RED EYED TREE FROG

They hide in the rain forest canopy and ambush crickets, flies, and moths with their long, sticky tongues.







SNAKES

There are many kinds of snakes that live in Rainforests including :

  • Venomous Snakes
  • Pythons
  • Boas
  • Tree Snakes
  • Anacondas






TWO TOED SLOTH

Sloths spend nearly all of their lives hanging from branches. Sloths even sleep in the trees, and they sleep a lot, 15 to 20 hours every day







GLOW WORMS

Mysterious glow worms were discovered in the Peruvian rainforest
