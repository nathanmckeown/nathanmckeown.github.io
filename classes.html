THERE ARE MANY DIFFERENT TRIBES OF PEOPLE THAT LIVE IN RAINFORESTS



HOW MANY?

There are almost 500 tribes who live in the Amazon Rainforest

About fifty of these tribes have not had any contact with the outside world

There are approximately 180 different languages spoken by these tribes.







TRIBAL FACTS

Men do the hunting. They hunt animals such as deer, monkey and tapir with blow guns and poison tipped darts.

Women do all the cooking, chores and taking care of the children.

People in the tribes dont wear much clothes.







FACE PAINTING

Members of the tribes like to paint their faces and their bodies.

They will also wear colourful masks and wigs.
