LEARN MORE ABOUT RAINFORESTS



Amazon

The most famous Rainforest is the Amazon Rainforest and it has the 2nd biggest river in the world , the Amazon River.

Congo

The Congo Rainforest is the 2nd most famous Rainforest in the world. It is found in Central Africa and is the 2nd largest Rainforest in the world.

Danger

Rainforests are some of the most beautiful places on Earth but can be one of the most dangerous places on Earth.

Did you know?

About 3000 people die in Rainforests a year.These people probably died of hunger or of the deadly creatures that live in Rainforests.

Layers

There are 4 layers of a Rainforest, the Emergent Layer, the Canopy Layer, the Understory and the Forest Floor.

Worry

Rainforests used to cover 14% of the Earth's surface but now they only cover 6% because people cut the trees down.
