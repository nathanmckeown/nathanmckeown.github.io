FACTS ABOUT THE TITANIC



Size

The Titanic was the world’s largest passenger ship when it was built. The Titanic was 269.1 metres long - 882 feet 9 inches.

Building

The Titanic was built in Belfast at the Harland and Wolff shipyard

Power

The Titanic burned around 600 tonnes of coal a day – hand shovelled into its furnaces by a team of 176 men

Danger

2 people were killed just making the Titanic.
